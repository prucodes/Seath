About Myself lol

Boasting much? nah, just stating facts tbh.

Me, Soriya Kim Eath - a poet, activist, photographer and a very hardworking student ( I really am ) am a twenty-year-old pursuing a Bachelor degree in Social Work at George Mason University. I am both a full-time student and an employee for George Mason University, working as a Swim Instructor for the Aquatic and Fitness Center. In my spare time, I enjoy volunteering for a variety of organizations that advocate for individuals who are living in poverty.

I started a non-profit photography service recently and am accepting donations to make better the lives of Cambodian children and the elderly who are living in poverty. I am planning to visit Cambodia during the winter break and take advantage of my stay there to distribute clothing and apparel, toys, school supplies, and much more to people in need.

I also enjoy going on adventures and sharing my fun to the world with the help of my camera. I enjoys the outdoors, especially the beach and have a deep love for nature. I am goofy and comedic and always try to make people laugh, HAHA.
